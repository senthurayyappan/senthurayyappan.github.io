Start your blog post with an engaging introduction that hooks the reader. This should be 2-3 sentences that explain what the post is about and why it matters.

## Main Section

Break your content into clear sections with descriptive headings. Each section should focus on one main point or idea.

### Subsection

Use subsections to break down complex topics into more manageable pieces.

## Images

Use `FloatImage` to let the following paragraphs flow around an image. `side` accepts `left` or `right`; `shape` accepts `box`, `soft`, or `circle`. On small screens it automatically becomes a full-width figure.

<FloatImage
  src="/default.png"
  alt="Describe the image"
  caption="Optional caption"
  side="right"
  shape="soft"
  width={640}
  height={640}
/>

The paragraphs after the component will wrap around it on wider screens.

## Conclusion

End your post with a strong conclusion that summarizes the main points and provides a call to action or next steps for the reader.
